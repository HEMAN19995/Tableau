## Netflix Dashboard

This Tableau dashboard provides insights into the Netflix movie and TV show catalog, including:

* **Overview of the Netflix catalog:** See the total number of movies and TV shows available on Netflix, as well as the distribution of content across different genres.
* **Top-rated content:** Discover the most popular movies and TV shows on Netflix, based on user ratings.
* **Trending content:** Stay up-to-date on the latest releases and most popular titles on Netflix.
* **Content by genre:** Explore the different genres of movies and TV shows available on Netflix, and see which ones are the most popular.
* **Content by country:** See which countries produce the most content for Netflix, and which countries have the most popular content on the platform.

This dashboard is a valuable resource for anyone who wants to learn more about the Netflix catalog and find new content to watch.

## How to Use the Dashboard

The Netflix dashboard is easy to use. Simply select the tab you want to view from the menu at the top of the page. You can also use the filters on the left-hand side of the page to narrow down the results.

For example, if you want to see a list of the top-rated movies on Netflix, simply select the "Top-Rated Movies" tab. You can then use the filters to narrow down the results by genre, country, or release year.
